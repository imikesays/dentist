1. Preventive Dentistry

Preventive dentistry is the cornerstone of maintaining good oral health. At Markham Gateway Dentistry, a variety of preventive services are offered to help patients avoid dental issues before they arise.

Routine Check-ups

Regular dental examinations are essential for detecting potential problems early. During these visits, dentists assess oral health, clean teeth, and provide personalized care recommendations.

Teeth Cleaning:

Professional cleanings are performed to remove plaque and tartar buildup, which can lead to cavities and gum disease. This service is crucial for maintaining a healthy smile.

Fluoride Treatments:

Fluoride helps strengthen tooth enamel and prevent decay. The clinic offers fluoride treatments, especially beneficial for children and those at higher risk for cavities.

Dental Sealants:

Sealants are a protective coating applied to the chewing surfaces of back teeth to prevent cavities. This service is particularly popular among parents looking to protect their children's teeth.

2. Emergency Dental Services

Dental emergencies can occur unexpectedly, and Markham Gateway Dentistry is equipped to handle urgent situations.

Same-Day Appointments:

The clinic offers same-day emergency appointments for patients experiencing pain, swelling, or trauma. This prompt service ensures that urgent dental issues are addressed quickly.

Root Canal Treatment in Scarborough:

One of the most common emergency services is root canal treatment. This procedure is performed to save a tooth that is severely infected or damaged. The skilled team at Markham Gateway Dentistry provides gentle and effective root canal therapy, alleviating pain and preserving the natural tooth.

Extractions:

In some cases, a tooth may need to be extracted due to severe decay or damage. The clinic offers safe and comfortable extraction services, ensuring patients are well-informed and cared for throughout the process.
